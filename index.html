OUR STEP-BY-STEP PROCESS

01

Washing

Fresh ginger rhizomes were washed thoroughly under running water to remove soil and impurities.

02

Grating

Cleaned ginger was finely grated to break the cells and release the juice rich in gingerols.

03

Straining & Squeezing

Grated pulp was placed in muslin cloth and firmly squeezed to extract the juice. The fibrous residue was discarded.

✅

Fresh Ginger Extract Collected

The strained liquid (ginger juice/extract) was collected in a clean container.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the fresh extract last?

3–5 days in refrigerator. Can be frozen for up to 3 months.

Do you offer bulk and custom orders?

Yes, we can supply according to your required quantity, packaging, and branding needs.

Key Chemical Aspect

Our cold grating & straining method preserves higher amounts of 6-Gingerol, the main bioactive compound, with minimal conversion to 6-shogaol.
